About Kottayam

Kottayam, the land of lakes, letters, and latex, is known for its literacy, beautiful backwaters, and lush greenery. It is a major hub of education and publishing in Kerala.

Top Attractions

  • Kumarakom Backwaters
  • Illikkal Kallu
  • Thirunakkara Mahadeva Temple
  • Vembanad Lake
  • Poonjar Palace

Culture & Festivals

Kottayam is home to traditional festivals such as Onam, Vishu, and cultural celebrations at temples. It is also famous for its vibrant literature and print media.
